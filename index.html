OUR MISSION

USSOCOM develops and deploys elite special operations forces to conduct global missions with the Joint Force, U.S. government agencies, allies, and partners, supporting operations against state and non-state threats to protect and advance U.S. interests.

CORE VALUES

Honor. Earn trust by doing what is right Courage. Stand firm against danger Excellence. Strive for the highest standards Creativity. Finding innovative solutions Respect. Value teammates and partners
